Adoption

Re-homing pets is one of the most crucial aspects of our work. Not only does it give one of our rescue pets a new life in a loving home, but at the same time, it makes room at the Centre for another pet that needs our help. Our aim is to always re-home pets within Cavite. If you think you can provide a happy, healthy, and safe home in Cavite for one of our rescue pets, please Contact Us about adoption.

Why Adopt?

  • Save a life
  • Find a loving companion
  • Reduce overpopulation
  • Enjoy the joy of pet ownership
