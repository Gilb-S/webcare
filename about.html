Welcome to Pet Adoption and Care Cavite,

In 2015, my friends and I started feeding stray animals in GMA, Cavite, driven by our shared passion for animal welfare. We formed 'HAGC - Humanity for Animals GMA Cavite' and focused on areas with high stray populations, like Paliparan. We volunteered at shelters, events, and rescues, including a two-week stint in Taal. During the pandemic, we continued to secretly feed strays, placing food on posts when no one was around. After COVID-19, we were encouraged to establish a shelter due to our growing number of rescues. In 2021, we opened our doors to provide a safe haven for stray and rescued animals. Today, we remain dedicated to caring for those in need.
